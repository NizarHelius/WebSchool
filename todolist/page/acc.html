About E6SCHOOL

Our Mission

At E6SCHOOL, our mission is to train the next generation of leaders, innovators, and changemakers. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for shaping the future. That’s why we offer programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical, real-world applications.

Our Valeus

E6SCHOOL is built on a strong foundation of values that guide every aspect of our teaching and community life. We believe in **Excellence**, pushing every student and educator to aim high and achieve their best.

Our History

Since our founding in 2010, E6SCHOOL has grown into one of the most respected institutions in the region. We started with a vision to create a new kind of educational experience — one that is modern, inclusive, and impact-driven.

Information Systems Management

Duration: 2 years

Degree: Master's

This program offers an in-depth education in IT infrastructure management and digital transformation. Students will explore key concepts such as enterprise architecture, data governance, cybersecurity, and cloud integration. By blending technology and strategic thinking, graduates are prepared to lead IT projects and drive innovation within complex business environments.

Human Resource Management

Duration: 18 months

Degree: Bachelor's

This program equips future HR professionals with modern strategies for managing talent and organizational development. It covers recruitment, performance management, labor law, and employee engagement. Emphasis is placed on emotional intelligence and leadership to ensure that graduates are ready to foster positive, productive, and sustainable work cultures.

Explore Brochure

Digital Marketing

Duration: 1 year

Degree: Certificate

Dive into the world of digital strategy and online brand communication. This program explores SEO, social media campaigns, data analytics, and content creation. With a focus on the latest marketing trends and platforms, students will gain practical skills to help companies grow and engage customers in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

Frequently Asked Questions

Admission is based on application review (CV, cover letter, transcripts) followed by a motivational interview. For some programs, technical tests may be required.
